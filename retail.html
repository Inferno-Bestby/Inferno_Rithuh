About BestBy

BestBy is an online platform that serves as a platform between the sellers and the consumers providing a dynamic pricing for perishable food with respect to their shelf life. It lets the retailer dynamically match the prices of perishable food to their value.

BestBy is aimed at minimising the food wastage due to expiry by giving more exposure to the amazing offers provided sellers to the customers.
