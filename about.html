We are BestBy

Not every food prepared is eaten. Food is not a commodity that is available in surplus. Millions of people die due to starvation. Nearly one third of the food produced in the world for human consumption every year gets lost or wasted. 40 percent of the fruits and vegetables, and 30 percent of cereals that are produced are lost due to inefficient supply chain management and do not reach the consumer markets. BestBy serves as an online platform between the sellers and the consumers providing a dynamic pricing for perishable foods.â€‹ It lets the retailer dynamically match the prices of perishable food to their value.

BestBy aims at not wasting food which is discarded due to "expiry" or "shelf-time". This platform is maintained specially for for selling food that is about to expire. Since consumers will have a limited amount of time to eat the food before it gets expired, sellers give away the food at a lower cost to the customers. Eventhough this offer being put up in their markets, customers often fail to notice them. This platform is intended to give proper exposure to such food. Customers also have a benefit here: food items that were costly could be now bought at an affordable price. In short, BestBy tries to minimise wastage of food, which can both provide more food to the deprived, as well as lesser food waste to manage and dispose.

About BestBy

BestBy is an online platform that serves as a platform between the sellers and the consumers providing a dynamic pricing for perishable food with respect to their shelf life. It lets the retailer dynamically match the prices of perishable food to their value.

BestBy is aimed at minimising the food wastage due to expiry by giving more exposure to the amazing offers provided sellers to the customers.
